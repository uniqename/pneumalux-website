Terms & Conditions

1. Scope of Service: PneumaLux will provide on-site mocktail preparation, full bar setup & teardown on a table or counter, premium decorations & garnish station, and self-serve bar presentation. No individual bartending (guests serve themselves). Client is responsible for providing venue, table/counter space, final headcount 2 weeks before event, and ensuring guest safety.

2. Liability: PneumaLux is not responsible for guest injuries, property damage, or events beyond our control (weather, venue issues, etc.). Client maintains venue liability insurance.

3. Travel: Travel outside 50-mile radius of downtown Columbus incurs additional travel fee (see invoice). Standard service includes setup within Columbus area.

4. Setup Requirements: Venue must provide table/counter space (minimum 6ft), power outlet within 25ft, and climate control. Outdoor events require weather contingency plan.

5. Final Headcount: Final guest count due 2 weeks before event. Changes within 2 weeks may incur additional fees.
