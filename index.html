Questions?

Everything you need to know

How far in advance should I book?

We're open and ready to book! Send us an inquiry at any time and we'll reach out to confirm feasibility. The sooner you contact us, the better we can customize your experience. We recommend confirming at least 2 weeks before your event.

What's your service area?

We proudly serve Columbus and the greater Columbus area within 50 miles of downtown. For events outside this radius, we offer travel services (additional travel fee applies).

Do you travel outside Columbus?

Absolutely! We travel beyond the 50-mile radius. A travel fee will apply based on distance and duration. Inquire about your specific location and we'll provide a custom quote.

When do you need final headcount?

Final headcount is due two weeks before your event. This gives us time to order ingredients, prepare custom menus, and ensure a flawless experience for every guest.

Can I customize the menu?

Yes! Custom menu design is included in our Luxe, Prestige, and Elite packages. We work with you to create drinks that match your theme, preferences, and event vibe. Include menu requests when you submit your inquiry.

What's included in the service?

All packages include full bar setup, teardown, mocktail preparation, professional bartender service, and premium presentation. Your package level determines hours of service, menu customization, and garnish options. See our pricing tiers for specific inclusions.

What about guests who want alcohol?

PneumaLux specializes exclusively in premium, alcohol-free mocktails. All our drinks are non-alcoholic by design. We do not provide alcohol or mixed-option services.

What's your cancellation policy?

30+ days before: Deposit refunded, can reschedule (max 2 reschedules). Less than 30 days: Deposit forfeited, balance refunded. Rescheduling: Only 2 reschedules allowed; balance payment due regardless. Complete details in our Service Agreement.

How do I secure my booking?

Submit your inquiry, and we'll send you a Service Agreement. To finalize your booking, a 50% deposit is due within 3 days of signing. The remaining 50% is due 14 days before your event. We accept Zelle, CashApp, Card, or Check. Once deposit is received, your date is confirmed!

Still have questions? We'd love to hear from you.
